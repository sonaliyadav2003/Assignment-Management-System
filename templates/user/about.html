What is Assignment Management System

Assignment & Projects management help teachers to assign tasks and deadlines to students and monitor their performance on completion of these tasks. An assignment, in general, is a project or piece of work that is assigned to someone as part of a job or study course. The assignment can take various forms such as homework, essays, dissertations , case studies, class presentations, statements of purpose, reports, thesis, term papers, semester-end papers or language analysis papers.
